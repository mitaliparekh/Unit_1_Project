Industry Trends



In this section, we will discuss some key industry trends.


    Abbreviation and Emoticons

    Abbreviation such as OMW (on my way) is used in e-mail and text communication to expedite the message process (Morley, 2014).

    Emoticons are used to express the tone of voice such as sarcastic, joking, pleasant, angry, etc. (Morley, 2014). 😄


    Desktop Virtualization

    The data is located in a virtual setting rather than a physical device. This allows the user to access their data from any device (Morley, 2014).


    Tiny PCs

    They are small personal computers (PC) on a USB drive. One can use such PCs to access data such as streaming services, music, etc. (Morley, 2014).
